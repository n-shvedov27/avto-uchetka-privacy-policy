Privacy Policy for AvtoUchetka

Last updated: 03.12.2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the AvtoUchetka mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.
All data you enter is stored locally on your device.

The App may contain the following user-generated data:

Records of vehicle maintenance

Dates, mileage, and service details

Photos of receipts, service stations, or vehicle parts

This data remains exclusively on your device unless you choose to back it up through third-party systems (e.g., iCloud).

2. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

Camera / Photos — to attach images to your maintenance records.

Permissions are used only for their direct purpose and never for data collection.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device.

The App does not transmit, upload, or analyze any information on external servers.

You are responsible for backing up your data using your device's built-in backup tools (iCloud, iTunes, etc.).

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children and does not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document with an updated date.
